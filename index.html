About Us

Data Berlin Meetup is Berlin's vibrant community of data professionals, enthusiasts, and learners, bringing together over 2,200 members passionate about data. We are a non-profit initiative, organized by volunteers who love working with data and sharing knowledge with the community.

What We Do

We organize regular meetups, workshops, and networking events focused on data-related technologies and techniques. Our events cover a wide range of topics including:

  • Data Analytics & Business Intelligence
  • Data Engineering & DataOps
  • Data Visualization
  • Machine Learning & AI
  • Big Data Technologies
