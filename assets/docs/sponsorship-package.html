About Data Berlin Meetup

Data Berlin Meetup is Berlin's vibrant community of data professionals, enthusiasts, and learners. With over 1,800 members, we bring together the city's data community through regular meetups, workshops, and networking events. We are a non-profit initiative, organized by volunteers who love working with data and sharing knowledge with the community.

Our Audience

  • Average event attendance: 80-120 professionals
  • 60% Individual Contributors
  • 20% Students and career switchers
  • 20% Managers and Directors
  • Representing 100+ companies in Berlin
